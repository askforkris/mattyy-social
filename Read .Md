Mattyy Social

Mattyy Social is a community-driven social platform designed to connect responsible people within local areas. The platform encourages safe ride-along companionship, navigation awareness, and social interaction as a way to reduce real-world aggression and isolation.

Instead of traditional social media that focuses only on posts and entertainment, Mattyy Social focuses on community responsibility, communication, and cooperation.

Users can create profiles, connect with others in their area, message safely, and organize ride-along activities with trusted individuals who understand the local environment.

⸻

Vision

Many conflicts happen when people feel disconnected or unsupported. Mattyy Social aims to create a social outlet that reduces tension by promoting connection and accountability.

The goal is to build a network where:
	•	Responsible drivers can offer ride-along companionship
	•	People can safely navigate their city with someone who knows the area
	•	Communication replaces aggression
	•	Local communities become more cooperative and connected

⸻

Core Features

User Accounts

Users can register and create profiles that identify their interests and local area.

Messaging System

Private messaging allows users to communicate before meeting or coordinating ride-alongs.

Ride-Along Connections

Users can connect with responsible drivers who know the area and can safely navigate.

Community Awareness

The platform encourages users to be aware of their surroundings and engage with their local community.

Moderation System

Admins can monitor activity and maintain a respectful and responsible environment.
